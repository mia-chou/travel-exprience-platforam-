Why Leeds?

Why Visit Leeds?

Leeds combines rich history, independent culture, and vibrant local experiences, making it one of Yorkshire's most exciting cities to explore.

The Oldest Commercial Railway

Established in 1758, the Middleton Railway holds the distinction of being the world's oldest continuously working railway. Originally built to transport coal from the Middleton Colliery to the markets of Leeds.

Step into Leeds' fascinating history, filled with the magic and splendour of a bygone era. Explore the industrial past, where the heritage of steam and coal comes alive with every chuff and whistle.
